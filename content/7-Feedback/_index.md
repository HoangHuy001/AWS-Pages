### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**
- The working environment at FCJ is professional while still being friendly and welcoming. The mentors and team members are always willing to provide support whenever I encounter difficulties, which helped me integrate into the team quickly and work more effectively. In addition, the well-organized working environment made it easier for me to stay focused. It would be even better if there were more networking activities or team-building events to strengthen the connections among participants.

**2. Support from Mentors / Team Admin**
- My mentor closely followed my learning progress and assigned tasks, providing clear guidance and answering my questions in detail. Instead of simply giving me the answers, my mentor often encouraged me to think independently by suggesting different approaches to solving problems. The team admin was also very responsive in providing documentation, announcements, and assistance whenever needed, making the internship experience smooth and well-organized.

**3. Relevance to My Academic Major**
- The tasks and activities throughout the program were highly relevant to my major in Information Technology. They allowed me to apply the knowledge I had learned at university while also exposing me to new technologies, tools, and real-world workflows that I had not experienced in the classroom.

**4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development**
- The program helped me improve both my technical knowledge and soft skills. I had the opportunity to learn about cloud computing services, gain hands-on experience with modern tools, and strengthen essential skills such as teamwork, time management, problem-solving, and professional communication. The valuable advice shared by my mentor also helped me build a clearer career direction.

**5. Company Culture and Teamwork**
- What impressed me the most was the collaborative and supportive culture within FCJ. Everyone was willing to share knowledge, exchange experiences, and help one another overcome challenges. This positive and open environment encouraged me to communicate confidently and continuously learn from those around me.

**6. Benefits and Support for Interns**
- The program offered many valuable learning opportunities through workshops, seminars, and knowledge-sharing sessions. In addition, the flexible schedule and continuous support from mentors made it easier for me to balance my academic responsibilities with the internship. Overall, FCJ provides an excellent environment for students to gain practical experience and develop themselves.

### Additional Questions

**What was the most satisfying part of your internship?**
- The most satisfying part of my internship was the opportunity to work in a professional environment, receive dedicated guidance from my mentor, and apply my academic knowledge through practical assignments and real-world projects. Throughout the program, I could clearly see my improvement in both technical expertise and professional skills.

**What do you think could be improved for future interns?**
- I believe the program could be further improved by organizing more experience-sharing sessions with industry professionals and providing additional group-based practical activities. These opportunities would allow interns to exchange ideas, collaborate more effectively, and strengthen their teamwork skills.

**Would you recommend this program to your friends? Why?**
- Yes. I would definitely recommend the First Cloud Journey program to my friends because it provides an excellent learning environment for students. The program not only helps participants improve their technical knowledge but also offers valuable hands-on experience, networking opportunities, and career guidance.

### Suggestions and Future Expectations

**Suggestions for improving the internship experience**
- I hope the program will continue to organize more in-depth workshops on Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Artificial Intelligence, while also providing additional real-world projects and collaborative activities. These experiences would allow interns to apply their knowledge in practical scenarios and better prepare for future careers.

**Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?**
- Yes. If given the opportunity, I would be delighted to continue participating in First Cloud Journey activities to expand my knowledge, improve my professional skills, and contribute more to the community.

**Additional Comments**
- I would like to express my sincere gratitude to the entire FCJ team, mentors, and administrators for their continuous support throughout my internship. This has been a truly valuable experience that helped me grow both professionally and personally. I hope the program will continue to develop and provide meaningful learning opportunities for future generations of students.
