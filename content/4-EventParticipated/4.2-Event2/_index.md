# Reflection Report: “First Cloud AI Journey 30/5”

### Purpose of the Event

- Share learning experiences and skill development in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence.
- Introduce topics related to DevOps, AWS Cloud Quest, and Hackathons.
- Enhance soft skills such as confidence, problem-solving, and teamwork.
- Strengthen the First Cloud AI Journey community through practical sharing sessions.

- **Why We Always Need Confidence**

### Key Highlights

#### Why We Always Need Confidence

- Confidence is an essential factor for success in both learning and professional work.
- Be proactive in asking questions, expressing opinions, and sharing ideas.
- Build confidence through continuous learning and real-world experiences.

#### The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster

- Many project issues originate from hidden problems rather than visible ones.
- DevOps improves software development and deployment workflows.
- The importance of CI/CD, automation, and monitoring in reducing project risks.

#### Hackathon Experience

- Shared practical experiences from participating in Hackathons.
- Learned how to collaborate effectively under time constraints.
- Understood the importance of task allocation and delivering an MVP within a limited timeframe.
- Gained valuable lessons in teamwork and problem-solving.

#### AWS Cloud Quest and Floci

- Introduction to the AWS Cloud Quest learning platform.
- Learning AWS through a gamified experience.
- Guidance on practicing AWS services from beginner to advanced levels.
- Shared a recommended AWS learning roadmap for students.

#### The Iceberg of Procrastination

- Analyzed the real causes of procrastination.
- Understood that laziness is often only the visible symptom.
- Learned effective time management strategies and ways to overcome procrastination.

### Knowledge and Skills Gained

#### Soft Skills

- Improved confidence in presenting ideas.
- Enhanced communication and teamwork skills.
- Learned effective time management techniques and how to reduce procrastination.

#### Technical Skills

- Gained a deeper understanding of DevOps and the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC).
- Learned the benefits of CI/CD pipelines and automation.
- Discovered an effective AWS learning approach through AWS Cloud Quest.
- Better understood the value of Hackathons in developing practical technical skills.

#### Career Development

- Realized the importance of balancing technical expertise with soft skills.
- Became more motivated to participate in community activities and expand professional networks.

### Practical Applications

- Apply DevOps practices to personal software projects.
- Participate in Hackathons to improve problem-solving and teamwork skills.
- Use AWS Cloud Quest to practice AWS services through hands-on learning.

### Event Experience

Participating in the **First Cloud AI Journey Sharing Day** provided me with valuable knowledge in both technical and soft skill areas. The event was more than just an AWS sharing session—it was an opportunity for community members to exchange learning experiences, discuss project development, and inspire one another to grow professionally.

#### Most Memorable Takeaways

- The **Why We Always Need Confidence** session helped me realize that confidence is the foundation for personal growth and creates more opportunities in both academic and professional environments.
- **The Hidden Iceberg of a Project: DevOps Before Disaster** introduced a new perspective on software development, emphasizing that major project failures often stem from unstandardized processes hidden beneath the surface.
- The **Hackathon** presentation inspired me with its stories of creativity, teamwork, and the ability to build a functional product within a short period.
- The **Level Up Your AWS Skills with Cloud Quest and Floci** session introduced an engaging and practical way to learn AWS through gamification and hands-on challenges.
- **The Iceberg of Procrastination** helped me understand the underlying causes of procrastination and provided practical methods to overcome it.

#### Community Networking

- Had the opportunity to connect with members of the First Cloud AI Journey community.
- Learned from experienced speakers who had participated in numerous projects and technology competitions.
- Gained a better understanding of career development paths in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

#### Lessons Learned

- Technical knowledge and soft skills should be developed together.
- DevOps is not only a set of tools but also a mindset for efficient software development.
- Hackathons provide an excellent environment for improving practical technical skills.
- AWS Cloud Quest is an effective platform for learning and practicing AWS services.
- Continuous learning, knowledge sharing, and active community participation are essential for personal and professional growth.
